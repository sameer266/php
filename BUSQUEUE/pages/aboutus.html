About Us

Welcome to BusQueue, a platform designed to make bus travel easier and more efficient. Our mission is to provide real-time information about bus routes, departure and arrival times, and feedback from passengers.

Our Mission

At BusQueue, we aim to improve the public transportation experience by offering a simple, user-friendly service that helps passengers plan their trips better.

Our Vision

We envision a future where bus travel is more organized, with accurate information at passengers' fingertips, making commuting easier and more accessible.
